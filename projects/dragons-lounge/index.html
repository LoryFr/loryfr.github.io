Dragon's Lounge - Logo Design

Designing the logo for Dragon's Lounge, where Magic: The Gathering is played, I integrated Smaug's eye, evoking an aura of mystique and grandeur that embodies the essence of the game and the fantasy realm. The use of amber color pays homage to the shades of treasure, symbolizing the richness of the gaming experience within.
